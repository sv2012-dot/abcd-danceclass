# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 2026

## 1. Introduction

ManchQ ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates as a comprehensive dance school management platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for:
- ManchQ web application (https://manchq.app)
- Associated services and APIs
- Communications related to our services

## 2. What Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Account Registration & Authentication:**
- Email address
- Full name
- Profile picture (via Google OAuth)
- Password (hashed and encrypted)
- School/Organization affiliation
- User role (Admin, Teacher, Parent)

**School Information:**
- School name and location
- Owner/Administrator name and contact information
- Phone number
- City and address
- Dance style specialization
- Bank account information (for fee processing)

**Student & Class Information:**
- Student names, ages, contact information
- Guardian/parent information
- Class enrollment and batch assignments
- Attendance records
- Performance and progress notes
- Emergency contact information

**Communication Data:**
- Messages and support tickets
- Feedback and survey responses
- Email communications

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Authentication & Session Data:**
- JWT authentication tokens (stored securely)
- Login timestamps
- Session information
- Device information (browser, OS, IP address)

**Usage Data:**
- Pages visited and features used
- Time spent on different sections
- Actions performed (creating students, scheduling, etc.)
- Error logs for troubleshooting
- Analytics through Google Analytics

**Technical Information:**
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Device type
- Referring website

## 4. Third-Party Services & Data Sharing

### 4.1 Third-Party Integrations

**Google Authentication:**
- When you sign in with Google, we receive: email, name, and profile picture
- Google's privacy policy governs their data collection: https://policies.google.com/privacy

**Email Service Provider:**
- We use Nodemailer/Gmail SMTP for sending transactional emails
- Welcome emails, notifications, and communications are processed through email services
- Email providers have access to email addresses and basic message content

**Cloud Infrastructure:**
- Database and application hosting on Railway
- Your data is stored securely on their servers
- Railway's privacy practices: https://railway.app/privacy

### 4.2 Data Sharing

We **do NOT** sell, trade, or rent personal information to third parties.

We share information only with:
- **Service providers** who assist us (email, hosting, payments) under confidentiality agreements
- **School administrators** within your school for legitimate educational purposes
- **Parents/Guardians** regarding their enrolled students (as determined by school administrators)
- **Legal authorities** when required by law or to protect safety

### 4.3 Parental Access

Parents can view information about their own children:
- Class schedules and enrollment
- Attendance records
- Event participation
- Communications from instructors
- Account information associated with their child

Parents cannot view other students' information.

## 5. Data Retention

- **Account Data:** Retained as long as your account is active. Upon deletion, most data is removed within 30 days
- **Backup Data:** Retained for up to 90 days for disaster recovery
- **Transactional Records:** Retained for 7 years (tax/legal compliance)
- **Analytics Data:** Retained for 13 months (Google Analytics default)
- **Payment Information:** Never stored on our servers (handled by payment processors)

## 6. Security

### 6.1 Security Measures
- **Passwords:** Hashed using bcryptjs with 10 salt rounds
- **Authentication:** JWT tokens with 7-day expiration
- **Encryption:** HTTPS/TLS for all data transmission
- **Database:** Parameterized queries prevent SQL injection
- **Access Control:** Role-based permissions (Admin, Teacher, Parent, Superadmin)
- **Rate Limiting:** API rate limiting to prevent abuse

### 6.2 Limitations
While we implement industry-standard security measures, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your password.

## 7. Your Rights & Choices

### 7.1 Access & Portability
- You can request a copy of your personal data
- You can download your data in a portable format

### 7.2 Correction & Deletion
- You can update your account information anytime
- You can request deletion of your account and associated data
- Some data may be retained for legal/tax purposes

### 7.3 Marketing Communications
- You can opt out of marketing emails (transactional emails continue)
- Unsubscribe links are provided in marketing emails

### 7.4 GDPR & CCPA Rights
If you are in the EU (GDPR) or California (CCPA), you have additional rights:
- Right to access your data
- Right to be forgotten (with exceptions)
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Right to withdraw consent

**To exercise these rights:** Contact support@manchq.com with your request and verification information.

## 8. Children's Privacy

ManchQ is a B2B service for schools and educational institutions. We do not knowingly market to or collect information from children under 13 without parental consent through their school's administrator.

Parents/guardians maintain control over their children's information through the parent portal.

## 9. International Data Transfers
